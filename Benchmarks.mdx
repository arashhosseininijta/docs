<Update label="2025-05-07 Benchmarks" description="Voice Harbor V3.0">

  **Simple sample**  
  **Format:** wav  
  **Channel:** 1 
  **Speaker:** 1
  **Language:** English
  **Duration:** 0:51 sec
  
  | task                  | Duration                   | Jon parameters | 
  |-------------------------|----------------------------|----------------------------|
  | transcribe | 14.12 sec     | ```job_params = {"files": [],"task":"transcribe"}``` |
  | phi     | 24.10 sec     |```job_params = {"files": [],"task":"phi", "model":"mini"}``` |
  | biometric            | 75.12 sec                    |```job_params = {"files": [],"task":"biometric"}``` |
  | phi-biometric            | 75.09 sec                    |```job_params = {"files": [],"task":"phi-biometric"}``` |

</Update>
